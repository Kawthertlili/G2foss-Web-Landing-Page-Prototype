About US

Thanks for your interest. Here's a quick story about us.

G²foss is a free and open-source software club founded in 2008 that operates within the National School of Engineering of Tunis (ENIT). The club's objective is to promote the culture and philosophy of free and spread the spirit of sharing.

Our Events

¤ Enit Got The Flag

The event "ENIT GOT THE FLAG 2.0" is a national competition for cybersecurity enthusiasts, which will take place on April 13, 2024, at the National School of Engineers of Tunis. This "Capture The Flag" (CTF) competition returns with even more intensity for its second edition. Bright minds in cybersecurity will come together to face stimulating challenges, testing their problem-solving skills and understanding of vulnerabilities. "ENIT GOT THE FLAG 2.0" is not just a competition, but a celebration of community spirit in the field of cybersecurity.

¤ League of Coders

The League of Coders, a national event for programming competition, is back with full force for its third edition. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their talent in problem-solving and compete for exceptional prizes.
